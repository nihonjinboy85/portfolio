About Me

My name is Richard. I am a self-taught web developer in Kingston, WA (near Seattle). Ever since I played Hangman on an old Mac as a kid, I’ve loved computers.

It wasn’t until a couple of years ago, however, when I found my passion for computer science. One of my co-workers suggested that I might like computer programming since she felt that I ‘have the brain for solving complicated problems’ - she was right (thanks, Carmen!). I took a few courses in web development and immediately knew that this was what I wanted to do with my life. Since then I have made time to continue learning despite working a full-time job, making improvements to the house that my wife and I just bought, and raising our three boys. I have learned a bunch of different languages but my favorites are Java, PHP, and Python (you can see a full list of the courses I have taken here).

But it’s not all work. I also go to Sounders soccer matches, do obstacle course racing, hiking, camping, playing video games, and messing around with Rubik’s cubes.

I currently am responsible for all improvements and maintenance on www.triad-orbit.com as well as www.accessbagsandcases.com as part of my "day job". However, I'm always looking for new opportunities, so if you like what you see and would like to start a conversation, send me an e-mail!

About Me

Richard Carrigan has been working as the in-house Web Developer for his company's two Wordpress e-commerce sites for the past few years, in addition to being the Logistics and Operations Manager since 2012. Although his professional experience has mainly focused on logistics and inventory management with a minor amount of web development work, he is now looking to shift careers into the tech industry, specifically front-end web development.

Most recently, Richard has been developing single-page web applications using Angular and React. He is skilled in a wide range of web technologies from HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through to more advanced languages such as TypeScript, SASS, and PHP. He also has limited experience with back-end languages, such as Node.js, Express, SQL, and MongoDB.

Richard's highest priorities when creating web properties, no matter the size, are:

  1. User-Focused,
  2. Mobile-First,
  3. Fast,
  4. and Engaging
